- Ultrawide is for Ultrawide monitors with an uncorrected aspect ratio.

- Dual_screen is for playing dual screen games such as "Rastan Saga Episode 3" and "Sagaia" on standard 16:9 monitors with a corrected aspect ratio (8:3).

- Triple_screen is for playing triple screen games such as "Darius" on standard 16:9 monitors with a corrected aspect ratio (12:3).
